About Our School

Shree Tilaurakot Secondary School, located in Kapilvastu Municipality-6, Gobari, is committed to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence. Our institution has been serving the community with dedication and passion.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures creativity, innovation, and academic excellence while preserving cultural values.
