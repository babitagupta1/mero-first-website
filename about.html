Our History

Shree Tilaurakot Secondary School, located in Kapilvastu Municipality-6, Gobari, has been serving the community with quality education for many years. Our institution has grown from humble beginnings to become a center of academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures creativity, innovation, and academic excellence while preserving cultural values.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Character Development
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Community Service
  • Cultural Preservation
